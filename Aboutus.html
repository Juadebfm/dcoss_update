About Us

DCOSS Associates Limited is a full-service treasury management company. We provide treasury strategy, deal structuring and restructuring services for "NHIs" - High net worth individuals and corporate entities. Clients can leverage our suite of services to put their financial assets to good use. Likewise, they can utilize it to hedge against currency devaluation and contribute their quota to a strong economy.
Through our first-hand knowledge and accumulated expertise in the global financial market, we provide strategic investment insights. Our clients enjoy extensive investment advisory, which includes brokerage and investment solutions which we execute in collaboration with our global strategic partners.

Since we began operating in 2012, we have repositioned the company to be more agressive in business objectives or services delivery and as a result of that we have facilitated over ₦10 billion in transactions from March 2022 to October 2022 and continue to do more as our client base increases. In 2015, we were certified by the Nigerian Financial Intelligence Unit (NFIU) of the Economic & Financial Crime Commission (EFCC). We have developed a reputation for our transparent structure and strategic solutions, which are all in complaince with corporate government requirements. Our clients include foreign portfolio investors, commercial and merchant banks, and asset management companies.
