Who We are

DCOSS Associates Limited is a proudly African treasury management company. We offer private and corporate clients a suite of financial services aimed at safeguarding and optimizing their assets. Established in 2012 and duly registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC), we have facilitated over ₦5 billion in transactions over the last six months across the global capital market.

What We Do

With our rich treasury background in the domestic and international financial scene, we help our clients to hedge their private and corporate assets against devaluation and domestic currency risks.

We leverage quantifiable market insight for sound investments. When you choose DCOSS Associates Limited, you are choosing a collaborative treasury management company that will expedite your finances.

We drive the success of assets sales, share sales and deal brokering. Just the same, we facilitate special purpose vehicles (SPVs) for organizations looking to expand globally through deal structuring, and we find investors to finance them.

We maximize business value so buyers offer our clients fair price at the point of sale. And if negotiations get tough, you can trust us to navigate it deftly. Our high success rate is proof.

We educate, socialize, and connect corporate entities with financial institutions that trade their assets in profitable financial instruments. To this end, we increase the value-add within these entities and from them to shareholders.

We cater to the needs of widows and children in developing countries. We create inclusions by providing them with shelter, education and other financial support. Similarly, we support indigenious missionaries in their work.
