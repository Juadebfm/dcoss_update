Frequently Asked Questions

What is the full meaning of DAL?

DAL and the logo "D" with a golden dot stands for DCOSS Associates Limited, A organization focused on treasury management and strategy, business process engineering and re-engineering, deals structuring and humanitarian services.

Does DCOSS Associates deal with individual clients?

Yes. DCOSS Associates' wholesale/institutional clients include pension fund administrators (PFAs), insurance companies, banks, other large financial institutions, and broker-dealers. We also work with individual clients and can occasionally work on contracts between two entities.

What is the minimum amount for treasury bill in the Nigerian market?

Treasury bills are highly liquid negotiable instruments, that are available in both primary and secondary markets. In line with the DMO guideline, the minimum amount of bid is N50,001,000.00 and in multiples thereof N1,000.
